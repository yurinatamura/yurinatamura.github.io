I co-founded a startup with a team of four during an intensive eight-week incubator program, focusing on personalized sensory toy subscription boxes for children with ADHD aged 4 to 17. I also led competitive analysis and developed strategies to differentiate our products in the market. Additionally, we achieved 100 presales prior to the pitch, demonstrating strong market interest and demand. Our efforts culminated in crafting a compelling pitch for demo day, winning the vote of four qualified investors, and securing 1st place in the competition.
